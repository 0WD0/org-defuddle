The company has officially announced the successor to its popular product today. After years of waiting, the new model is finally here.

** Available to order next week

The new model features a next-generation chip that enables improved performance across the board.

The chip also powers an upgraded noise cancellation experience. Per the company:

#+begin_quote
"With the incredible performance of the new chip, users get up to 1.5x more effective noise cancellation for the ultimate listening experience," said a company spokesperson.
#+end_quote

New features include:

- Live Translation
- Adaptive Audio
- Voice Isolation
- Gesture Controls

The new model supports high-resolution lossless audio when connected with the included cable.

It costs $549 and will be available to order starting next Wednesday.

The new model comes in five color options: midnight, starlight, orange, purple, and blue.
